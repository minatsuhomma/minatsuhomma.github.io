Mix It

The Theme

How might we create a joyful mixed reality experience which offers a recurring element of surprise?

What's Mix It?

Mix It is a cocktail mixing game, with which player can choose from three ingredients and pour them into a shaker. A combination of physical LED strip and Unity digital output on the computer screen will visualize the liquid level. Once the shaker is full, the computer mixes the cocktail and drinks it. The original cocktail is then rated by the computer.

How is it made?

The three ingredients are represented by Raspberry Pi which are connected to Sense Hats. While pouring the ingredients, the pouring angle is detected. The angle values are sent to Unity via Spacebrew, which acts as a connecting interface. In Unity, these values are mapped to the size of the shaker. Accordingly, the liquid level is digitally visualized on the screen and the corresponding amount of LEDs are switched on.
